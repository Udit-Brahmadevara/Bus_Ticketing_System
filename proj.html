About us!

In order to cater to the transport needs of the travelling public of the then state of Karnataka and Andhra Pradesh, Our company TUL was inaugurated with 12 buses on 12th September 1948.
Our goal is to provide safe, comfortable and affordable transport between the two states and be reliable.
